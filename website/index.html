Since 1957

Three generations of one Cedarburg family

Founder Willard "Willie" Geiger left his farm in 1957 and opened an agency on Western Road with one rule: our only product is service.

His son Gary took over in 1985 and spent decades building relationships with top-tier carriers and the best claim service money can buy. Today it's a third-generation family business — Gary, Ben, and Teddy Geiger — still in the heart of historic Cedarburg, still protecting families and helping them rebuild what they've worked hard for.

We don't want to be the biggest agency in Ozaukee County. Just the best. Gary, Ben, and Teddy are here to stare down risk and help you come up with a game plan to overcome it.
